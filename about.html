My Name is Kyle Meroney and my Hobby is Photography.

The main type of Photography that i enjoy is landscape photography and astro photography.

In recent years since my son has been involved in his high school marching band i have started shooting at football games and marching competitions.

I use a pair of Canon R6 Mark II's to catch all the action. I recently added a second Canon R6 Mark II to my setup to be able to catch close up action while i am shooting on the football field.

My R6 Mark IIs are paired with a Canon RF 70-200mm f/4 L IS USM lens and a Canon RF 24-105mm f/4 L IS USM Lens. This Combination of camera bodies and lenses ensure i am able to capture wide shot while also being able to reach out and capture images closeup.

A intersesting photograhy trip that i took was when i traveled to the Mcdonald Observatory in the Davis Mountains. I was able to capture the Mily Way in the dark skys of the Davis Mountains.
